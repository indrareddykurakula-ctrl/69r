ROHIT SHARMA

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian international cricketer and the captain of the India national team in ODIs. He is also a former captain in Tests and T20Is. He is widely regarded as one of the greatest opening batters of all time, and one of the best batsmen of his generation.[3] He is a right-handed batsman who plays for Mumbai Indians in Indian Premier League and for Mumbai in domestic cricket.
